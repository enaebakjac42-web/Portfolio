Who I am

Committed, reliable, and always where it matters.

Since September 2024, I have been working as a commercial assistant at KNDS CBRN in Saint-Chamond, a French defence company that exports CBRN protection equipment to government and industrial clients across Europe and the Middle East. It is a demanding, highly regulated environment that has made me rigorous, precise and genuinely comfortable navigating complex B2G and B2B processes. This is where theory became practice.

What drew me to international business is the same thing that draws me to literature: the need to read between the lines, to understand what is not said, and to find the right words for the right context. Whether I am drafting a commercial proposal in English or recommending a market entry strategy, the underlying skill is the same — listening carefully before speaking.
