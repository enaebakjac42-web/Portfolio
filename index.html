I like to understand before I act, read between the lines and go to the heart of things.

I am a third-year Business Techniques student specialising in International Business at IUT Jean Monnet, Saint-Étienne. A semester at ESB Business School in Reutlingen and an apprenticeship at KNDS CBRN have genuinely changed the way I see international commerce.

Work experience

  • Follow up on international orders administratively and logistically (ADV cycle)
  • Read and review commercial proposals in English for international defence clients

  • Support trade fair preparation and internal logistics
  • Communicate daily in English with teams and external partners

What I've learned: rigour, cross-functional collaboration and a real understanding of how international trade works in a high-stakes environment.

Four months that changed my perspective.
